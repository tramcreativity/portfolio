HI THERE!

My name is Tram (/trum/ - like “gum”!) and I am a graphic and interactive designer based in Orlando, FL. I have a background in the hotel industry in Vietnam, but I have always been involved in crafting and design work as a hobby. When I moved to the US, I decided to apply for the Graphic & Interactive program at Valencia College, Orlando. I found this program to be a perfect blend to let my artsy personality and design skill grow while keeping up with the future of technology.

When it comes to graphic and interactive projects, I enjoy learning about my target audience and prioritize the end users’ needs to develop the ultimate design solutions. This work is perfect for me because I enjoy solving puzzles as my pastime! I can take on many design challenges like layout, typography, or photo editing; but my favorite projects are logo design, branding campaigns, web design and UI/UX.

During my spare time, I enjoy reading, playing puzzles, working on some craft projects, hanging out at theme parks(I’m a big fan of Harry Potter and Disney!), or visit animals exhibits. I also like to travel and explore new cultures around the world.
