Shepherd's Hope 25th Anniversary

Shepherd’s Hope is a non-profit clinic providing free health care for individuals and families who need medical care and have nowhere to turn. It was founded by Dr. Williams S. Barnes in 1997. The organization is holding a Masquerade Ball with a “Night at the Museum” theme and they are in need of a variety of assets. They would also like to introduce their 25th Anniversary logo that will be used by them for the next 5 years.

I was in charge of designing the anniversary logo and associated items for the event.


Category: Rebrand; Poster Design
Tools: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Indesign
